Ponea Health Patient Onboarding

In 2022, I designed the flagship mobile app for Ponea Health, a digital healthcare startup.

The new Patient App aggregated a number of services launched by Ponea Health over the past two years.

This meant that users would now find all their services in this one "super-app".

How it worked

With the patient microservice, we grouped all login credentials and patient data belonging to one user using a unique Ponea ID.

This ID made it easier to isolate everything about that patient: email addresses, phone numbers, medical records, payment records, appointments, etc.
